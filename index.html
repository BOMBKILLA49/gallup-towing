Gallup New Mexico Towing Dispatch

Gallup Towing is a towing dispatch and coordination service connecting you with trusted, licensed local towing and roadside assistance providers throughout New Mexico. We are not a direct towing company and do not operate our own fleet. When you call us, we take your information, arrange payment, and dispatch a qualified local provider to your location — so you get fast, reliable help without the hassle of finding someone yourself.

  • Roadside Coordination — Fast dispatch of local roadside assistance providers.

General FAQ'S

Common questions about our dispatch service

Are you a direct towing company or a dispatch service?

Gallup Towing is a dispatch and coordination service — we are not a direct towing company and do not own or operate our own trucks. When you call us, we arrange payment and connect you with a licensed, independent local towing or roadside assistance provider in your area. All physical services are performed by third-party operators.

How long does it take for a provider to reach me?

Response times depend on your location and local provider availability. In most areas we serve, providers typically arrive within 30 to 60 minutes. We will give you an estimated arrival time when we dispatch.

What does a tow usually cost?

A tow is quoted by your location to your destination, as well as type of vehicle and condition. Give us a call for a free quote today.

Can I have my vehicle towed from out of state to NM?

Yes — as a dispatch service we work with providers across multiple states and can coordinate long-distance towing to or from New Mexico. Give us a call and we will arrange everything for you.
