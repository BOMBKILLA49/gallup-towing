Roadside Assistance

Flats, jumpstarts, fuel delivery, lock-outs — coordinated for you

We coordinate fast, courteous, and affordable roadside assistance services throughout New Mexico. Gallup Towing is a dispatch and coordination service — we are not a direct towing company and do not operate our own trucks or equipment. When you call us, we connect you with a trusted, licensed local provider who can reach you quickly and safely, no matter the situation. Whether you're stranded on I-40, I-25, or a remote stretch of highway, we dispatch local professionals 24/7 for common roadside emergencies including flat tire changes, dead battery jumpstarts, emergency fuel delivery, and vehicle lock-out assistance. All physical services are performed by independent, third-party operators in our network. We handle the coordination and payment so you get fast, reliable help without the hassle of tracking someone down yourself.

  • Quick Tire Changes
  • Swift Jump Starts
  • Fuel Deliveries
  • Vehicle Lock-Outs
  • Available 24 hours a day, 7 days a week

General FAQ'S

Common questions about our dispatch service

Are you a direct towing company or a dispatch service?

Gallup Towing is a dispatch and coordination service — we are not a direct towing company and do not own or operate our own trucks. When you call us, we arrange payment and connect you with a licensed, independent local towing or roadside assistance provider in your area. All physical services are performed by third-party operators. For oversize loads, pilot cars are coordinated through our network of certified providers as required by state regulations.

How long does it take for a provider to reach me?

Response times depend on your location and local provider availability. In most areas we serve across New Mexico and the west, providers typically arrive within 30 to 60 minutes. We will give you an estimated arrival time when we dispatch.

What does a tow usually cost?

A tow is quoted by your location to your destination, as well as type of vehicle and condition. Give us a call for a free quote today.

Can I have my vehicle towed from out of state to NM?

Yes — as a dispatch service we work with providers across multiple states and can coordinate long-distance towing to or from New Mexico. Give us a call no matter where you are located and we will arrange everything for you.
